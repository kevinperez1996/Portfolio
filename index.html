About Me

My current career is in Social work, where I am working in the foster care department with Turning Points for Children. I began working in the field after graduating with a bachelor's degree in 2017 from the Pennsylvania State University. After Graduating I entered my current field of work unintentionally after being recomended into the field by a close relative.

Friends and family consider me to be a "tech wizard", a term to which I laugh, since I am aware of the extensize size of the "tech world". I'm no tech wizard, however I do consider myself a "gear head". On the weekends and during my free time, I like to work on cars, mostly changing the look or performance of the cars. I am also into gaming, a hobby that I no longer can entertain as much as I would like to due to my busy schedule.

My spouse and I got married in December of 2016 after knowing eachother for years. We now are both continuing our education while trying to maintain full time jobs aswell as take care of a very small and very needy dog named Ollie. I hope to one day celebrate our accomplishments by taking a vacation to Bora Bora or perhaps have some dinner on the Eiffel Tower while struggling to understand the locals.
